Atlas Messaging

Atlas is a new blast messaging app that allows you to create a location-based page to message many people at once. People nearby can follow your page to receive messages as a notification, like a text, and can also privately message you.

Atlas leverages the Turbo Datastore and Email API service to notify users for certain announcements, events, and matters of local interest.

02

Velocity 360

Velocity 360 is a membership-based website where users learn to program web and mobile apps using React, Redux and Node. In addition to selling online courses, Velocity 360 operates in-person classes in New York City.

The Velocity 360 website uses Turbo for much of the data persistence and payment functions. All payment charges are processed through the Turbo SDK and the blog system runs from Turbo as well. The newsletter is handled through the Turbo email service to update the over 600 registered users of Velocity 360.

Rob Ungar Portfolio Site

Rob is a full stack Node, React, and Redux developer based in New York City. He works as a consultant for early stage startups and digital marketing agencies. He published a portfolio site on Turbo as a quick, convenient way to deploy a site with little overhead.

Rob's site only uses the hosting feature of Turbo which means there are no requests for data or any of the service APIs. As a result, Rob is hosting his site completely for free.
